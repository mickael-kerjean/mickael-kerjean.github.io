Nuage

A Dropbox like experience for your FTP/SFTP/S3/Minio/ server

Manage your data
Nuage lets you manage your data regardless of where it is store and whatever this data is. It connects to FTP/SFTP/S3/Minio/ and many other.

Flexible Collaboration
Thanks to its flexible sharing mechanism, nuage allows the creation of shared links with fine grain access control. Collaboration links can be mounted as a network drive, handy!

Configurable and customisable
Nuage can be configure to fit many use cases. Need additional flexibility? Create your own plugin

Note taking
Thanks to it’s powerfull editor, nuage is a fully feature org mode client which mean you can use it to create notes, agendas, todos and even author beautiful documents

Your photo gallery
Upload your photos and share a link to your friends. The image viewer expose all the metadata in your pictures

Multimedia viewer
Nuage can read your videos and music straight from your browser
